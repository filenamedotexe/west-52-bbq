## 3. Catering Services

### 3.1 Booking and Reservations

- **Additional Charges**: Last-minute changes to guest count or menu may incur additional fees.

### 3.3 Cancellation and Refund Policy

- **Catering Cancellations**:

- **Quality Issues**: If you experience a quality issue, please contact us immediately at (779) 702-9004. We will work to resolve the issue promptly.

### 3.4 Guest Count Changes

- **Final Headcount**: Must be provided **72 hours before the event**. You will be charged for this confirmed number.
- **Increases**: Guest count increases of more than 10% may not be accommodated depending on our capacity.
- **Decreases**: Reductions in guest count **less than 72 hours before the event** will still be charged at the confirmed amount.
